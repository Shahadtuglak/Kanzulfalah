ABOUT US

(السلام عليكم)

Asalamualaikum

Kanzulfalah the term in it self means "The treasure of Success". What is Success? In today's world it means to live a lavish life,earning money,touching the height of success. Is it? No as far as we are Muslim's our Success is to make our life lives according to Our Beloved Prophet ﷺ. As it is stated in Quran:-

SUCCESS LIES ONLY WITH ALLAH. -Quran(11:88).

Kanzulfalah connects You with ALLAH and his Messenger by which your worldly life (Duniya) and here after (Akhira) will be Successful.

Kanzulfalah have several functions on Islamic Days. Which creates a harmony among people and it connects each and every body. Whether its 12th Rabi-ul-Awwal to 6th of Rajjab. Every Islamic day is celebrated. (Alhamdulilah) And the second thing is the Kanzulfalah classes which is Unique in itself.

It's not a normal Arabic classes which will teach Quran. No!! it's main aim is to make People read the Quran properly with "Tajweed".

Classes will teach&learn you the followings:-

1. Learn Quran with Tajweed.

2. Learn Proper way of Namaz.

3. Learn Sunnah of Prophetﷺ..

4. Learn All the basics rule of Islam.

5. Learn Dua for every activity of Life.

6. Learn to respect your Parents&Elders.

7. Learn to Love and Respect People.

By This you will be Successful once you Join the Classes and become a part of Kanzulfalah.. (In sha ALLAH)
